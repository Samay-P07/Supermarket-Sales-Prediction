SUPER MARKET INFORMATION

Supermarkets are food retailers that offer a full line of grocery items, produce, meat, seafood, canned goods, general merchandise (non-food items), health and beauty items, and may offer bakery, deli, or pharmacy services. These stores can range in size, carrying between 15,000 to 16,000 product SKUs.

The India grocery market size is estimated to increase by USD 289.29 billion and grow at a CAGR of 8.06% between 2022 and 2027. The rise in disposable income among consumers in India is the key factor driving the market growth.
